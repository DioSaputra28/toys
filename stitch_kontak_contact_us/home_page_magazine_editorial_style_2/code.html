OUR STORY

It began in a small garage in 1985. Tired of the fragile, plastic toys flooding the market, our founder Elena set out to create something different—toys that felt good to hold, smelled like real wood, and could withstand the wildest adventures.

What started as a collection of simple wooden cars has grown into a curated boutique of dolls, puzzles, and playsets. But our core philosophy remains unchanged: simplicity is the ultimate sophistication.

Every piece in our boutique is hand-selected from artisans who share our values of sustainability, ethical production, and timeless design.
